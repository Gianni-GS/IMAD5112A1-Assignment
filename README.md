# IMAD5112A1-Assignment
This is my app to help Cora to maintain social connections during busy days by suggesting social "sparks" or actions based on the time of day.
To properly use the chatbot app, you need to enter the correct time of day e.g ( Morning ). After entering the correct time of day you need to press the "press button" 
on the app and you should get a valid response. If you enter the incorrect time of day then you will get a invalid result. Press the "reset button" to start over. Once 
you enter the correct time of day then you will get the correct result.
